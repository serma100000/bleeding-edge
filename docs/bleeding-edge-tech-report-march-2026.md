## 2. QUANTUM COMPUTING

### 2.1 Majorana Qubit Parity Readout
- **Who:** QuTech / Delft University | **Date:** February 12, 2026
- **What:** First-ever single-shot parity readout of Majorana qubits via quantum capacitance. Parity coherence >1 millisecond. Published in Nature.
- **Impact:** Solves the fundamental readout problem for topological qubits. Validates Microsoft's Majorana 1 approach.

### 2.2 Lattice Surgery on Superconducting Qubits
- **Who:** Published in Nature Physics | **Date:** February 6, 2026
- **What:** First demonstration of lattice surgery -- splitting one logical qubit (17 physical) into two entangled logical qubits while continuously correcting errors.
- **Impact:** Workhorse technique for fault-tolerant quantum computing now proven on real hardware.

### 2.3 QphoX Quantum Transducer (Commercial)
- **Who:** QphoX (Netherlands), IBM first customer | **Date:** March 12, 2026
- **What:** Commercial product converting microwave ↔ optical quantum states. Enables quantum info over standard telecom fiber.
- **Impact:** Missing link for distributed quantum computing. Production-grade, not lab-only.

### 2.4 D-Wave On-Chip Cryogenic Control
- **Who:** D-Wave | **Date:** January 2026
- **What:** First scalable on-chip cryogenic control of gate-model qubits. Controls tens of thousands of qubits with just 200 bias wires.
- **Impact:** Solves the wiring bottleneck for superconducting quantum computers.

### 2.5 Fermilab/MIT Cryoelectronic Ion-Trap Control
- **Who:** Fermilab / MIT Lincoln Lab / DOE | **Date:** February 2026
- **What:** In-vacuum cryoelectronics for ion-trap quantum computing. Solves wiring bottleneck for the ion-trap architecture.

### 2.6 Google AlphaQubit -- AI Error Correction
- **Who:** Google DeepMind | **Date:** Early 2026
- **What:** 2.14x scaling factor on Willow processor. 30% fewer errors than best traditional decoders. ASIC integration for real-time use.
- **Impact:** Validates the Threshold Theorem. Quantum systems now improve with scale. End of the NISQ era.

### 2.7 QuantWare VIO-40K -- 10,000-Qubit Architecture
- **Who:** QuantWare (Delft) + NVIDIA NVQLink | **Date:** Announced late 2025; shipping 2028
- **What:** 3D chiplet architecture enabling 10,000-qubit processors (100x current). 40,000 I/O lines. Single cryostat.

### 2.8 Triplet Superconductor Discovery (NbRe)
- **Who:** NTNU Norway | **Date:** February 21, 2026
- **What:** NbRe alloy transmits electricity AND electron spin with zero resistance at 7K. First confirmed triplet superconductor.
- **Impact:** Could dramatically stabilize qubits while cutting energy consumption.
